Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯
Our Mission

🌟
Our Vision

💎
Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Accountability, Innovation, Collaboration, and an unwavering commitment to Africa-centred solutions that respect cultural context and local knowledge.

Our Story

Built for African Realities

Tuakwa Consultancy Hub was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between strategic intent and real-world impact across Africa. Based in Accra, Ghana, we draw on a deep reservoir of regional expertise and international experience.

We understand that sustainable peace and development require more than good intentions — they require integrated strategies, robust institutions, and policies grounded in evidence. That is exactly what we deliver.

What We Do

Our Services

Four core service domains, each designed to address the most critical challenges facing African institutions and communities today.

🕊️
Service 01

Service Consultancy in Peace & Security

We provide strategic advisory and solutions to enhance peace, security and stability across fragile and conflict-affected contexts. Our approach is grounded in African realities and informed by decades of on-the-ground experience.

  • Conflict analysis and early warning systems
  • Peace negotiation support and mediation
  • Security sector reform advisory
  • Disarmament, Demobilisation and Reintegration (DDR)
  • Community peacebuilding and reconciliation programmes
  • Strategic risk assessments for organisations operating in fragile states
🎤
Service 02

Conferencing Support

We offer professional, end-to-end support for conferences, seminars, workshops and high-level events. From logistical coordination to content facilitation, we ensure every event achieves its strategic objectives.

  • High-level diplomatic conference management
  • Workshop and seminar facilitation
  • Technical secretariat services
  • Rapporteur and documentation services
  • Stakeholder engagement and participant management
  • Post-event reporting and follow-up coordination
🔬
Service 03

Research & Policy Development

We conduct rigorous, evidence-based research and develop actionable policies that inform critical decisions and drive lasting impact. Our policy work spans governance, security, development, and humanitarian response.

  • Conflict and political economy analysis
  • Policy briefs and position papers
  • Programme evaluation and impact assessment
  • Legislative and regulatory review
  • Strategic planning facilitation for institutions
  • Data collection, analysis and visualisation
♟️
Service 04

Strategic Domains Integration

We integrate critical strategic domains to create synergy, build resilience and achieve transformative results. This holistic approach ensures that peace, governance, and development objectives reinforce each other.

  • Cross-sector strategy development
  • Institutional capacity building and training
  • Multi-stakeholder coordination frameworks
  • Governance and public administration reform
  • Development programme design and management
  • Monitoring, evaluation and learning systems
How We Work

Our Process

01
Discovery & Assessment

We begin with a thorough understanding of the context, stakeholders, and challenges through desk research and field engagement.

02
Strategy Design

We co-create tailored strategies with clients, ensuring ownership, cultural relevance and alignment with long-term objectives.

03
Implementation

We deploy our expertise on the ground, coordinating with partners and stakeholders to deliver quality outcomes on time.

04
Evaluation & Learning

We measure impact rigorously, capture lessons learned, and adapt continuously to ensure lasting transformative change.
